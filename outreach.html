Community Partners

Support received from community partnerships allows Friendship Medical Clinic to achieve its mission and improve the health of our community.

Conway Medical Center and Conway Medical Center Foundation

Conway Medical Center and Conway Medical Center Foundation partner with Friendship Medical Clinic to assist with the costs of hospital services, rent, utilities and medical supplies. Conway Medical Center Foundation also supports preventive health screenings for Friendship Medical Clinic patients.

Friendship Medical Clinic has been a United Way of Horry County Partner since 1974. The continual financial support from the United Way has allowed this clinic to serve the community for over 50 years. Friendship Medical Clinic partners with other United Way agencies to improve the community as a whole.
