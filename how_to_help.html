How You Can Help

Semper amet scelerisque metus faucibus morbi congue mattis

There are many ways that you can help Friendship Medical Clinic & Pharmacy in its mission to provide basic, primary medical care and medications to low income and underserved adults in Horry County who have no access to medical insurance. Whether you are volunteering your time or skills, filling up our supply closet or supporting our work with outright monetary donations, please know that we could not and would not exist without your help. By supporting our work, volunteers and donors can help save lives everyday!

Here is a brief "wishlist" of items that we keep in the waiting room to give to our Patients:

  • Small household items: pots and pans, dishes
  • Washed clothing and shoes
  • Washed sheets and towels, blankets
  • Washed clothing for kids
  • Canned foods
  • Diabetes
  • COPD
  • Toys if not broken
  • Children's books if in good condition

This is a "wishlist" for items commonly needed in our clinic:

  • Paper towels and toilet paper
  • Hand sanitizers
  • Liquid hand soap
  • Napkins
  • Paper plates
  • Plastic forks, knives, spoons
  • 13 gallon size, white, garbage bags
  • Kleenex
  • Unopened pharmaceuticals
  • Drug samples that are not expired
  • Office supplies such as: copy paper (we use lots of copy paper), pens, paper clips, black or red magic markers, pencils
  • New coloring books and new crayons for our children's corner in the waiting room
  • Children's books for children's corner in the waiting room
  • $10 grocery store reward gift cards for nurses to give Patients as door prizes to those losing the most weight
